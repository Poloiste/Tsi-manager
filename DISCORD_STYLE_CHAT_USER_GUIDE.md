## Getting Started

### Accessing the Chat

1. Log in to TSI Manager
2. Navigate to the **ğŸ’¬ Discussions** tab
3. Click on **ğŸ’¬ Salons** to access the Discord-style interface

### Creating Your First Category

1. Click the **+** button in the sidebar header
2. Enter a category name (e.g., "Mathematics")
3. Choose visibility:
   - **Public**: Everyone can see channels in this category
   - **Private**: Only invited members can see channels
4. Click **Create**
5. A "General" text channel is automatically created in your category

### Creating Channels

1. Hover over a category in the sidebar
2. Click the **+** button next to the category name
3. Configure your channel:
   - **Name**: Give it a descriptive name
   - **Type**: Choose Text or Voice
   - **Visibility**: Public or Private
4. Click **Create**

### Chatting in Channels

1. Click on any channel in the sidebar
2. Type your message in the input field at the bottom
3. Press **Enter** or click **Send** to submit
4. Your messages appear on the right (in blue/indigo)
5. Other users' messages appear on the left

### Managing Private Channels

For private channels, you'll automatically become the owner when you create them. As an owner, you can:

1. Invite members (feature coming soon)
2. Assign roles to members (owner, moderator, member)
3. Remove members who shouldn't have access
4. Delete the channel if needed

## Tips & Best Practices

### Organizing Your Categories

- **By Subject**: Create categories for each subject (Math, Physics, Chemistry)
- **By Purpose**: Create categories for different activities (Study Groups, Homework Help, Projects)
- **By Team**: Create private categories for specific study groups

### Channel Naming

- Use clear, descriptive names
- Keep names short and concise
- Use lowercase for consistency
- Examples:
  - `general` - Main discussion channel
  - `homework-help` - For homework questions
  - `exam-prep` - For exam preparation
  - `announcements` - For important updates

### Privacy Considerations

- **Public channels**: Great for open discussions and community building
- **Private channels**: Perfect for team projects, sensitive discussions, or exclusive study groups
- Remember: Once a channel is created, changing visibility requires admin intervention

## Troubleshooting

### I can't see a channel

**Possible reasons:**
- The channel might be private and you're not a member
- The category might be collapsed - try expanding it
- You might not be logged in

**Solution:**
- Check if the category is collapsed
- Ask the channel owner to add you if it's private
- Ensure you're logged in

### Messages aren't appearing

**Possible reasons:**
- Network connectivity issues
- Browser issues with real-time updates

**Solution:**
- Refresh the page
- Check your internet connection
- Try clearing browser cache

### I accidentally created a channel in the wrong category

**Solution:**
- Contact an administrator to move the channel
- Alternatively, delete it and recreate it in the correct category
- (Direct channel moving feature coming soon)

## FAQ

**Q: Can I move channels between categories?**
A: Not yet - this feature is coming in a future update. For now, recreate the channel in the desired category.

**Q: How many channels can I create?**
A: There's no hard limit, but we recommend keeping it organized with 3-5 channels per category.

**Q: Can I delete messages?**
A: Yes! You can delete your own messages by clicking the trash icon that appears when hovering over your message.

**Q: What happens if I delete a category?**
A: All channels within that category will be deleted as well. Be careful!

**Q: Can I rename a channel after creating it?**
A: This feature is coming soon. For now, you'll need to recreate the channel with the new name.

**Q: How do voice channels work?**
A: Voice channel functionality is coming in a future update. For now, voice channels are display-only.
