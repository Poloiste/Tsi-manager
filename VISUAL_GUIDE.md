- **MÃ©ca:** Green to Emerald gradient (`from-green-600 to-emerald-600`)

- **Anglais:** Red to Rose gradient (`from-red-600 to-rose-600`)
- **FranÃ§ais:** Indigo to Violet gradient (`from-indigo-600 to-violet-600`)
- **Informatique:** Slate to Gray gradient (`from-slate-600 to-gray-600`)

### Chapter Rows
- Background: `bg-slate-900/50` with `border-l-2 border-slate-600`
- Hover: `hover:bg-slate-700/30`

### Flashcard Rows
- Background: `bg-slate-900/50`
- Hover: `hover:bg-slate-800/50`
- Text: White for question, Indigo for author

- Background: `bg-blue-500/20`
- Text: `text-blue-300`
- Border: Rounded corners
